Kenya Civic Leadership Roles

Governor:
- Heads the county executive
- Oversees county service delivery
- Implements county laws and policies

Senator:
- Represents county interests in the Senate
- Protects devolution
- Oversees revenue allocated to counties

Member of Parliament (MP):
- Represents constituency in National Assembly
- Makes laws and approves national budget

Member of County Assembly (MCA):
- Represents ward in County Assembly
- Makes county laws and approves county budget
